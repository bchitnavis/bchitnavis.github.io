Investing: Clarity Over Hype

I think about investing as a decision system, not a prediction game. The goal is to make a small number of rational choices and repeat them through changing market cycles.

My approach emphasizes asset allocation, risk awareness, and time horizon discipline. I prefer frameworks that are understandable, explainable, and maintainable over decades.

Most importantly, I treat behavior as part of portfolio design. A strategy only works if it is robust enough to follow when uncertainty is high.
